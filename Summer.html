About


Background

Summer was composed by Lu LiangHui in 1980. Lu LiangHui has composed four music piece with the theme "Four Season". Summer is the second music piece using the season as a theme. It is presenting the change of weather in summer and how the people feel with it. Summer is program music, the theme of this music piece is imitating the sound of nature. In this music piece, the audience can find that the musical instruments are imitating different sounds in nature (weather).

Structure


The whole music in general can be divided into three parts.



Part 1:

It is describing the hot weather in summer.

Part 2:

It is describing the weather during the storm in summer, including the coming of black clouds, thunder and rain.

There is a short rest between the second layer and the third layer to show the weather difference between 2 parts.

Part 3:

It is describing the weather after the storm. The tempo is slower than layer 2 with the soft melody played by Bangdi and Zhang means that the weather turns better now than layer 2 and sunshine comes after the storm.


Musical Instruments and Playing techniques

how instrument imitate storm in part 2

Large Drum

大鼓

The thunder was imitated by the percussion instruments, timpani, and the large drum. The musician beat the drum with different accent and tempo to simulate the sound of thunder.



Pipa

琵琶

The rain sound was imitated by Pipa. It is a plucked-string instrument. It used both hand techniques. The left-hand technique is called jiaoxian. It means tangle two strings together. The right-hand technique is called lunzhi. It means tremolo with five fingers. This two techniques combine similar to the sound of heavy rain.

Erhu

二胡

The wind sound was imitated by Erhu. It is a blowed wind instrument. It uses the techniques called zhangong to emulate the blowing of wind in a storm. Zhangong is mean tremolo. It is similar to the wind blow in the windows.



Underlying meanings of the music: (nature & human)


In layer 1, it shows that the weather in summer is hot and stuffy. Human feels annoyed.
After layer 2, the bad mood of human has washed away by the storm.
In layer 3, sunshine brings bright to human. Although summer brings bad weather to human, they still praise for the beautiful summer.
